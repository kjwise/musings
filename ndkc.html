Neurodivergence knowledge capture

Introduction

Hello, I'm Jóhann and I'm Neurodivergent, despite being high functioning. My first diagnosis was tourettes back in 1989. I've been fighting my Neurodivergence, accepting it, learning about it, over the years. Self-development, working out, making mistakes, figuring better out my needs and Boundaries. I've picked up many tools and a lot of knowledge on the way. I am not a physician or a medical expert. I'm here to provide knowledge as a peer.

Purpose

The purpose of this page is to provide useful information, such as supplementations, emotional release tools, the relationship between trauma and Neurodivergence, diet, relationships and more.

How to Use This Table

  • Focus on supplements rated A for the most significant potential benefits.
  • Use the Use With column to identify synergistic combinations.

    • Target Specific Effects

  • For Focus: Look at supplements with a ✔ in the Focus column.
  • For Calmness: Choose supplements with a ✔ in the Calmness column.
  • For Sleep: Select supplements with a ✔ in the Sleep column.

    • Additional Considerations

  • Choose high-quality products from reputable brands to ensure purity and potency.
  • Introduce new supplements one at a time to monitor how your body responds.
  • Keep a journal to track the effects on your focus, calmness, and sleep, adjusting the regimen as needed with professional guidance.
  • Supplements work best in conjunction with a healthy lifestyle, including a balanced diet, regular exercise, stress management techniques, and good sleep hygiene.
